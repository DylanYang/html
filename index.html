iHeadLab Research

iHeadLab devotes to research Interactive Courseware via AI technology.However, I believe that we need to look at more than just the problems that users encounter. We need to understand how users are approaching problems, what the strategies users employ with their assistive technologies to overcome or circumvent those problems. A highlight of my work that came from Yunnan Educational Administrition project A Research On Inte- grated Management Evaluation System of Minority Preparatory Instruction of Higher Education College Based on Visualisation Computing, where I developed a system that integrate Visualization and AI tecnology.Through applying the technology of visualization integrated with social network to track the trace of the students behaviors, building up the model according to the ontology semantic rules, and making use of automatic reasoning mechanism to generate visual graphics after extracting the data, it can provide the basis and support to the decision making on the managing solutions to the national preparatory students.
