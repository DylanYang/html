iHeadLab Research

iHeadLab is devoted to research Interactive Courseware via AI technology. I believe that as well as looking at the problems users envouter, we also need to understand how users approach the problems, what strategies users employ to overcome the problems. One of the highlight of my work was a research project I did for Yunnan Education Bureau entitledA Research on Management Evaluation System of Minority Preparatory Instruction of Higher Education College Based on Visualisation Computing, in which I developed a system that integrates Visualization and AI tecnology. The system assists decision making related to the management of students through a variety of technologies, ranging from tracking student behaviours throught applying visualization technology to social networking tools, creating models using ontology semantic ruels, to the generation of visual graphics using automatic reasoning mechanism.
