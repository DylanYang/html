iHeadLab Research

iHeadLab devotes to research Interactive Courseware via AI technology.However, I believe that we need to look at more than just the problems that users encounter. We need to understand how users are approaching problems, what the strategies users employ with their assistive technologies to overcome or circumvent those problems. A highlight of my work that came from my European project I2Web, where I led the team in understanding the strategies that people with disabilities use at a micro-interaction level which led to some pretty surprising results about the depth and range of strategies used by blind screenreader users. For more information on this work, check out the paper published at Interact in 2013 This link opens a new window..
